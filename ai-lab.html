🎤 VocalGuru

Compare your own singing against the original recording and track improvement over time. Both takes run through Splitter to isolate the vocals, then get analyzed for pitch accuracy, melodic contour, and consistency using librosa, Praat, and FFmpeg.

Token-gated while in active development — email admin@nuvam.ai for access.
